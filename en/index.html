A privacy-first safety platform MVP that demonstrates a heatmap, reporting flow, and dashboards for municipalities, regions, countries and continents.

Open Safety Map Open Dashboard
MVP demo only — no real emergency calls or live data storage.

What this MVP shows

  • Heatmap demo with dummy safety reports (privacy-by-design view).
  • Municipality dashboard demo (municipality → region → country → continent).
  • Panic button (UX placeholder) — visible, not functional yet.
  • Report unsafe location flow — adds a new entry to the heatmap instantly (client-side).

Why now?

Municipalities are increasingly looking for a central digital safety hub against gender-based violence. S4W can serve as an independent, privacy-first platform that enables visibility, reporting and decision support.

Core principles

  • Zero surveillance
  • Zero identity tracking
  • Privacy-by-default
  • Community-driven reporting with abuse-prevention layers
  • Designed to scale globally
